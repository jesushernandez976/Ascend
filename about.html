About Ascend Spine & Rehab

Located In St. Charles, IL

Welcome to Ascend

Our mission is to give you the tools to rise above the limitations of pain and reclaim a life where you no longer have to avoid the things you love. By uncovering the fundamental barriers holding you back and treating the root cause, we partner with you to deliver individualized care that empowers you to live fully and freely.

Meet Dr. Bruni

Dr. Shane Bruni graduated with his Doctorate in Chiropractic Medicine from National University of Health Sciences in Lombard, Illinois. Dr. Shane goes beyond simply treating symptoms. By taking a "whole person" and rehab-focused approach, the underlying imbalances affecting your strength, mobility, and stability are addressed. Dr. Shane will identify the root cause of your issues and create a personalized plan to not only alleviate pain but also elevate your athletic performance and overall health.

Meet The Team

When I’m not focused on helping you move and feel your best, I’m probably spending time with my beautiful wife Rachael and our son Ford. We’re also getting ready to welcome baby number two, which has us all busy, but excited.

Outside of family time, you can usually find me on the golf course, in the gym, out hiking or running, or just hanging out with friends.

My passion for chiropractic care and exercise rehab comes from a genuine desire to help people take control of their health and keep doing the things that bring them joy. I know how frustrating and limiting an injury can be — whether it’s missing out on playing with your kids, not being able to enjoy a favorite sport or hobby, or just struggling with everyday tasks.

That’s what drives me to provide care that’s not only effective but empowering. I’m here to help you understand your body, build strength and resilience, and get back to doing what you love.

I’m truly excited to support and serve the people of St. Charles, West Chicago, Wayne, Geneva, and surrounding communities.
